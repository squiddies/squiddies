ABOUT US

The thought process for this product has been very in depth and thorough. We had several factors and key components that we made sure was implementated during the prototyping process of it. From the convenience of it to the user-friendliness, we made sure that it would be appealing to our target audience, Being the ELDERLY. With these factors in mind, we had aims and goals that we hope to achieve in the future.

AIMS & LONG-TERM GOALS

Simplicity,Portability and Affordability are the key factors we made sure is well represented by our poroduct. With this in mind, we strive to give our users the utmost user-friendliness inter-face, no matter how "non-tech savy" they might be . This is defintely mandatory especially when our target market is the elderly. Big and easy-to-read fonts are essential in order for them to understand and illustrate what is on the screen.

The Future

All members in this team hopes to attain a certain goal through this product. An active and healthy environment islandwide. Through this simple app, we hope to motivate not only the younger generation but also the elderly. We believe that they have more potential in living a healthy lifestyle than they think they do.

As people get older, the risk of cotracting health diseases such as diabetes, high blood pressure, etc are heightened. However, studies have clearly proven throughout the years that trying to keep fit through doing physical activities as simple as jogging or breeze walking could potentially decrease the  chances of contracting such health problems and even improve mental health.Being physically active can help you stay strong and fit enough to keep doing the things you like to do as you get older. Making exercise and physical activity a regular part of your life can improve your health and help you maintain your independence as you age. For more information click here. As such in mind, we created this application to encourage the elderly to go out and enjoy life through excercising and being physically active. Taking it slow and easy is just the first steps too being fitter and more active.
